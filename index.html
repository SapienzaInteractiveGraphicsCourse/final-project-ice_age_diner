How to Play

  • 1. Host the customers: Click on the customer to select them.
  • 2. Assign a table: Click on an empty table to assign it to the selected customer.
  • 3. Take orders: Click on the ready customer to take their order.
  • 4. Serve a dish: Click on the plate on the counter and then on the customer to serve them their order.
  • 5. Plate to clean: Click on the dirty plate to grab it, even stacking it onto another plate and put it on the tray on the counter.
